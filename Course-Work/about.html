A quick glance at our store

Celebrate the World of Books at Themiya Store! Our passion for literature is at the heart of everything we do. Step into a welcoming and inspiring space where every book is a gateway to adventure, knowledge, and wonder. With a curated collection spanning across genres, we're dedicated to helping you find the perfect read. Our friendly, knowledgeable staff is always ready to guide you to hidden gems or the latest bestsellers. Whether you prefer the tactile experience of a physical book or the convenience of e-readers, we have something for everyone.

At Themiya Store, it's not just about selling books; it's about fostering a community of readers. Join us for author events, book clubs, and lively discussions, and become a part of our literary family. Explore, connect, and celebrate the magic of storytelling with us

Themiya Store opened its doors in 2003 with a simple yet profound mission: to connect people with the books that ignite their imagination, inspire their curiosity, and provide a window into new worlds. We started as a small, independent bookstore with a passionate team of bibliophiles who shared a common love for the written word.

Over the years, we've grown into a beloved community of readers, writers, and book enthusiasts. Our journey has been one of constant discovery, as we've expanded our offerings to encompass a wide variety of genres, authors, and formats.

Dive into Our Rich Collection of Literary Treasures! At Themiya Store, we take immense pride in our diverse and extensive collection. Our shelves are a tapestry of genres, from timeless classics to contemporary gems, catering to all tastes and preferences. You'll find captivating fiction, gripping mysteries, inspiring biographies, thought-provoking non-fiction, and a wide range of titles for young readers. We curate our selection with care, ensuring that each book tells a unique story and offers a new perspective. Our collection is not just a reflection of the literary world;

it's an invitation to explore different cultures, voices, and ideas. No matter your interests, you're bound to find a book that resonates with you at Themiya Store. Browse through our aisles or explore our digital catalog, and let the magic of literature transport you to new realms of imagination and knowledge. word.

At Themiya Store, Our Commitment Goes Beyond Books. We're more than just a place to buy books; we're a beacon of literacy and community engagement. Our commitment to the written word extends to fostering a love of reading in all its forms. We actively support local authors, offering a platform for their work to shine. We host book clubs and reading events that bring people together to discuss and celebrate the power of storytelling.

Furthermore, we're dedicated to promoting literacy and education. We believe that books are tools for personal growth and lifelong learning, so we frequently engage in outreach programs, partnering with local schools and libraries to provide books and resources to those in need.

Our store also embodies sustainability and ethical practices. We're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint, from eco-friendly packaging to sourcing responsibly printed materials.

In every facet of our operation, from community involvement to sustainable business practices, we're dedicated to making a positive impact. At Themiya Store, our commitment isn't just a statement; it's a way of life that ensures our role as not only a bookseller but a catalyst for positive change in our community and beyond.

At Themiya Store, our team is the heart and soul of our book-loving community. Comprising dedicated and passionate individuals, we share a common love for literature and a commitment to creating a welcoming and enriching experience for our customers.

Our knowledgeable booksellers are the guiding stars of our store. They're more than just salespeople; they are literary enthusiasts who are here to help you find the perfect book to suit your taste. Whether you're seeking a thrilling mystery, a heartwarming romance, or a mind-expanding non-fiction read, our team is always ready with insightful recommendations.

Our support staff works behind the scenes to ensure a seamless shopping experience, both in-store and online. From organizing events to managing inventory, they keep the gears of our literary world turning smoothly.

We're not just a team; we're a family of book lovers. We share book recommendations, discuss our favorite authors, and continuously educate ourselves about the ever-evolving world of literature.

Every member of our team is dedicated to making your visit to Themiya Store a memorable one. We're here to help you explore the endless realms of the written word, engage in thought-provoking conversations, and inspire your love for books. Come meet us in person at our store or interact with us online; we can't wait to be your literary companions on your reading journey.
